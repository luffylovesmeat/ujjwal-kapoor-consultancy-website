Meet Our Core Team

With years of experience in providing various accounting, bookkeeping, payroll & taxation services these are our core members

Ujjwal Kapoor

Mr. Kapoor, an alumnus of La Martiniere College, Lucknow with record of being a House Captain and awards for accounts in college; is a senior member of our team who has been in consultancy practice for Direct & Indirect Taxes since 1999 now for 19 years plus 6 year of training. He has experienced numerous changes in tax regime and is rich in experience for implementation of accounting system with changes in taxation regime. He has been associated with IIM-Lucknow since 2002 & IIM-Kashipur as service tax consultant. He was called to be the ‘Junior Tax Guru’ by Radio FM and has to his credit many opinions/write- ups for newspaper and interviews in FM radio. He has partnered 11 camps of public awareness and 02 conferences with Income Tax & Service Tax Department.

Mr. Arvind Kumar Dixit

He is a retired class one officer from Central Excise & Customs Department in 2016. He serves as Assistant Commissioner and is known for his interpretation and law abidance without bias judgments. He has major role in studying and implementing GST laws, transitional issues, tax planning and case studies. He also trains the team. He supervises drafting of replies to departments for notices and show cause up to appeal levels.

Mr. Manik Shah

He is a pass out of resident program of ICFAI in Finance from International Management Institute, New Delhi. He is a TRP (Income Tax) & STRP (Service Tax). He is an accredited Training Partner from NACIN. He has to his credit numerous GST trainings for more than 100 delegates and providing GST transition related consultancy to 25 firms in NCR. He was also a speaker in GST training at IIM-Lucknow for one day training held on 05/July/2017. He is also a master trainer of Election Commission of India. He mainly takes care of GST clients in Delhi & NCR and GST trainings. He is also experienced in GST returns and compliances, Income Tax return filing & assessments, Service Tax consultancy and return filings, accounting of manufacturing and service sector business houses. He is also a trained consultant for equity and Mutual Fund Investment consultant and Government of India Bonds.
